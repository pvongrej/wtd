Technology evolution by period

Era

This section divides my mobile journey into several technology eras. Each one shows how phones changed over time, from simple GSM devices to modern 5G flagships.

It is not only about hardware. Each era also changed the way I used my phone in everyday life.

First Mobile Phones

1998 to 2002

Simple GSM phones used mainly for calls and SMS. They were basic, durable, and had long battery life.

Alcatel One Touch Club+, Ericsson T10, Ericsson T28s

Golden Age of Button Phones

2003 to 2006

Classic button phones with more features, such as color displays, Bluetooth, simple games, and early cameras.

Nokia 6310i, Nokia 6230

Pre Smartphone Experimentation

2007 to 2009

A transition period with 3G, multimedia features, PDA style devices, and Windows Mobile experiments before smartphones became common.

Sony Ericsson K610i, HP iPAQ hx4700, Asus P750

Early Android Era

2010 to 2012

Touchscreen phones with apps, internet, navigation, and Android becoming the main operating system.

HTC One V

Android as the Main Device

2013 to 2016

Smartphones became everyday devices for communication, photos, maps, music, video, and social media.

Google Nexus 5, Samsung Galaxy S6

Mature Smartphone Era

2017 to 2020

Advanced smartphones focused on better cameras, stronger performance, improved displays, and user experience.

Samsung Galaxy S9, Samsung Galaxy S20

Modern 5G Flagships

2021 to 2024

Modern high end phones with 5G, strong performance, advanced cameras, and smart software features. This is the era where the phone became the device I use for almost everything, from photos and payments to navigation, entertainment, and everyday tasks. The improvements are not as dramatic as they were in the early smartphone years, but better displays, cameras, battery life, and software support still make a difference. Choosing the next phone now feels more complicated, because it is no longer just about the phone itself, but also about the whole ecosystem around it.

Samsung Galaxy S21, Samsung Galaxy S24
